About the Founder

George Cherian

Founder – Prime Trust Realtor

George Cherian is a seasoned professional with over 18 years of experience in banking, leadership, sales, and customer relationship management. After building a successful career in the financial sector, he brings his strong expertise in client advisory, negotiation, and strategic planning into the real estate industry.

As the Founder of Prime Trust Realtor, George specializes in helping clients buy, sell, and invest in residential properties across Mumbai, particularly in premium developments such as JP North and surrounding areas up to South Mumbai.

His strong financial background allows him to guide clients with transparent advice, market insights, and the best value deals within their budget. George believes that buying a property is one of the most important decisions in life. His approach focuses on trust, professionalism, and personalized service, ensuring every client experiences a smooth and confident property transaction.

With a deep understanding of property valuation, negotiation, documentation, and market trends, George works closely with buyers, sellers, and investors to create win-win opportunities in the real estate market.
